Street Safety Challenge

Scared of data? Contact us!

We are working with Cambridge City Council to curate policy-related questions that will inform the Council’s ongoing interest in improving public safety for pedestrians, bicyclists, and drivers in Cambridge, and specifically nearby schools.

Policy Questions and Data

Through this challenge, we hope to inform policy questions such as:

  • Where are unsafe intersections in Cambridge and why might this be?
  • What are safe routes for cyclists with low levels of comfort riding a bicycle to commute to work?
  • What are safe biking routes for children to schools?
  • Have a policy question? Add it here!

Judges and Awards

A grand prize award and a number of best-in-class awards will be awarded for participation, including most-creative application of data. These awards will come along with cash prizes and/or job offers and fellowship opportunities.
